User Data Deletion Instructions

App/Service: COM2PME
Effective date: 2026-01-16

Deletion request contact: contact@com2pme.fr

How to Request Deletion

If you want us to delete data associated with your use of COM2PME, follow the steps below. We will process valid requests as quickly as possible.

  1. Send an email to: contact@com2pme.fr
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request - COM2PME
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your full name (as shown on your Meta account)
    • The email address associated with your Meta account (if applicable)
    • Your Facebook User ID (if known) and/or the Page ID you connected (if applicable)
    • A brief description of what you want deleted (e.g., “delete all data”)

What We Delete

Depending on what the App stored for your account, deletion may include:

  • Stored access tokens (if any are stored)
  • Connected account identifiers (e.g., Page/IG Business IDs)
  • Configuration records associated with your account
  • Locally stored media references created by the App (if applicable)

What We May Retain

We may retain limited information when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes, or where retention is required by law.

Processing Time

We typically process requests within 7–30 days, depending on the scope of the request. If we need additional information to verify ownership, we will contact you.
